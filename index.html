About Us

Ganpat Grand Hotel is promoted by Mr. N. Hariharamuthu. It has been over 27 years since Mr. N. Hariharamuthu, with an experience of more than 2 decades in the field of hospitality laid out his vision to provide excellent quality of service to the pilgrims of Palani. His mission is to achieve consistent customer satisfaction and also to develop properties in various pilgrim cities. Ganpat Grand Hotel caters to premium segment that provides all facilities at one stop. This property is very close to temple with beautiful hill view and has 60 superior a/c room, 3 suite rooms, multi-cuisine restaurants, banquet halls, party halls and spacious parking. Hotel Ganpat is our budget hotel in Palani, offering over 52 rooms, multicuisine restaurants and other facilities.
